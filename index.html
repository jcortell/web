I've seen things you people wouldn't believe...

paraphrasing Roy Batty

I have advised a king to abdicate and establish a republic, I have been completely surrounded by snakes, I have survived earthquakes, hurricanes and floods, I have traveled through 5 continents circling the Earth over 30 times, I have been shot and poisoned and drown, I have overcome cancer, I've spent months in the hospital before I was even born, I have photographed archaeological excavations in a war zone and documented environmental disasters in the jungle, I have won international sports championships, I have created software that has been used to cure millions of patients, I lived with a severely ill psychiatric patient for years, I have been held by an anti-terrorist squad in Turkey, I have won awards in different scientific disciplines, I have dined in a palace with British Royalty, and lunched with ambassadors, I have served food to homeless people in Bangladesh, I have partied in California beaches with billionaires, and I've surfed in Ibiza, the girlfriend of a Colombian drug lord did a striptease for me in front of him in Bogota, I have gathered evidence about am organized crime group and handed it over to the police which led to prison sentences, I have organized demonstrations in the streets of New York and strikes in Spanish classrooms, I've had more than a dozen surgeries and I have suffered fractures in my neck (two) and all my limbs, I fled from the secret police during the performance of a censored theater play in pre-democracy Spain, the police thwarted a plan to kidnap me in Peru, I have been under so much stress that I have cracked five teeth, in Moscow my bodyguard (unbeknownst to me) stopped the traffic with an AK47, as a child I was beaten so badly that one of my eyes changed color permanently (heterochromia iridum), I developed software in collaboration with NASA to be able to give my fiance an extraterrestrial engagement ring and when it got lost I went down a volcano and got lava to make another, I've given lectures to thousands of people in a stadium and to dozens in Ivy League universities (Stanford, MIT and Harvard), I've seen birth and death, a policeman has stolen from me and a thief has helped me, I have climbed into the ring to fight in several martial arts, as a child during a coup d'tat I stared for hours through the window at a tank aiming towards my apartment threatening to shoot, the university that forced me to resign later gave me two medals, I've been buried in the snow, I've seen my son sink into a frozen lake, I've been so rich that I went on luxury cruises, and I've been so poor that I could not afford basic utilities, I've lived in a skyscraper in New York, a castle in London, a house on the lake in Canada, and a sea view mansion by the Med, I sailed on a fishing boat during a storm in the North Atlantic, a hurricane has lifted me off the ground, I have snowboarded in Canada during a polar vortex, I've cruised on a solar-powered catamaran... Above all, I've loved and been loved. I've found beauty.


And I'm only getting started... but all those moments will be lost in time,

like tears in rain.

Business

Venture Partner at NLC.health (The Netherlands).

CEO x7 in 6 industries. Entrepreneur x6 (5 exits) in 5 industries. I speak 3 languages fluently, traveled to over 50 countries in all continents, lived in 4. My last company was chosen among the Top 18 EU Startups, earning 4.9/5 stars and 100% approval rate as CEO from my coworkers and 85/100 (top in the industry) by my competitors and peers.

I have advised governments in Latin American countries, as well as the European Commission, IBM, Microsoft, and UBS bank, on matters related to copyleft, technology, innovation and healthcare.

If you want to know more, take a look at my business profile in LinkedIn.

Art & Activism

I've lobbied against software patents in the European Parliament, lectured against restrictive copyright throughout Europe and the USA, founded a citizen participation platform and Valencia's hacker lab, given lectures at hacker conferences, documented environmental abuses in Cambodia, copyright-lobby illegal activities in Spain, and police abuses in the USA, risked jail time as conscientious objector of compulsory military service...

I've curated art exhibits in different countries, have had some of my art works exhibited in some of the main museums and galleries around the world, and have also been an Advisor for the Saint Charles Royal Academy of Arts.

If you want to know more, take a look at my art profile in LinkedIn.

Science & Technology

Privileged to help build new health tech ventures at NLC.health, and having had the opportunity to play a part in advancing the field of Computational Bioinformatics for Precision Medicine at Kanteron as inventor of the first precision medicine software platform (2014), first vendor-neutral digital pathology dicomizer (2012), and pioneer developer of augmented reality via surface projection in live surgery (2009). Our technology helped save millions of lives and received innovation awards from: NASA, HIMSS, IBM, DPA, F&S, WSJ...

I've been invited to deliver Guest Lectures over 60 times in universities such as Stanford, Harvard, and MIT, and in events like HIMSS, RSNA, Longevity World Forum, etc. I have been assistant professor in 3 universities, and studied in 7...

If you want to know more, take a look at my profile in Orcid, ResearchGate, Google Scholar, or Academia.edu.
