Business

Member of the Board of Advisors at Lysando (Liechtenstein), world leader in the development of antimicrobial proteins. Also Technology Advisor and ex-Venture Partner at NLC.health (The Netherlands) where we have already built over 100 healthtech ventures, and I've personally built (in my portfolio): FlashPathology, Pryfiber, Matermend, PathBrite, GenoTell, WaveFront Microscopy, and OmicSystems, while also managed University accounts like Columbia, Harvard, NYU, CSIC...; and Strategic Advisor at Cross Ocean Fund (Silicon Valley).

CEO 6 times and Entrepreneur 6 (5 exits) in 5 industries (fashion, marketing, architectural tech, music production, and healthtech) in Spain, England and the USA. My last company, a Covid-19 victim, was chosen among the Top 18 EU Startups, bootstrapped via multi-million $ sales in 15 countries and profitable, being valued at $1.2B by a NY investment bank and receiving a buyout offer by a Japanese corporation, earning 4.9/5 stars and 100% approval rate as CEO from my coworkers and 85/100 (top in the industry) by my competitors and peers.

I have advised governments in Latin American countries, as well as the European Commission, IBM, Microsoft, and UBS bank customers, on matters related to copyleft, technology, innovation and healthcare.

I speak 3 languages fluently, traveled to over 50 countries in all continents, lived in 4.

If you want to know more, take a look at my business profile in LinkedIn.

Art & Activism

Hacktivism: I've lobbied against software patents in the European Parliament, lectured against restrictive copyright throughout Europe and the USA, founded a citizen participation platform and Valencia's hacker lab, given lectures at hacker conferences, documented environmental abuses in Cambodia, copyright-lobby illegal activities in Spain, and police abuses in the USA, risked jail time as conscientious objector of compulsory military service...

I have been an Advisor for the Saint Charles Royal Academy of Arts. I've curated art exhibits in different countries, edited and wrote the texts for Nebula's first photography art book 'Gaze, Reflexion, Fusion', and had some of my art works exhibited in some of the main museums and galleries around the world (Contemporary Art Museum of Santiago de Chile, Ludwig Museum in Cologne, Die Neue Sammlung and Haus der Kunst in Munich, Hermitage and Straat in Amsterdam, Konsthall in Malmö, Contemporary Art Museum of Copenhagen, and Saatchi Gallery in London).

Science & Technology

Former Innovation Fellow at Harvard University John A. Paulson School of Engineering and Applied Sciences (SEAS) Technology and Entrepreneurship Center at Harvard (TECH), and Expert in Residence at Harvard iLabs and Mentor at Harvard HealthLab.
