JORGE AS ART COLLECTOR

Jorge has been collecting for decades, having in his private collection works by artists such as K. Hokusai, D. Shrigley, D. Ciccirelli, O. Tezuka, C. Motta, P. Velver, P. Althamer, E. Kornilova, JV. Cascales, C. Zirotti, S. Fairey (Obey), etc.

JORGE AS ART CREATOR

Jorge's non-commissioned works have been exhibited at museums worldwide like The Hermitage and Straat (Amsterdam), Tate Modern Gallery and Saatchi Gallery (London), Copenhagen Contemporary, Malmö Konsthall, Die Neue Sammlung and Haus der Kunst (Munich), Ludwig Museum (Cologne), Contemporary Art Museum (Santiago Chile), etc.

Some of his works are for sale at Saatchi Art, and some of his projects are online at Tumblr.

JORGE AS ART CURATOR

As Independent Art Curator (ICI 826), in April 2016 Jorge curated (unrelated to his work as Advisor to the Saint Charles Royal Academy of Fine Arts) his first international art exhibition 'Net Freedom Art' with works by artists from Colombia, Poland, Japan, USA, Lithuania, Italy, Spain, and Bulgaria.

JORGE AS ART COMENTATOR

In June 2012 Jorge edited and wrote an essay for the art book 'Gaze, Reflexion, Fusion' about NEBULA's contemporary photography.

Jorge also writes about art regularly in his blog (tag and category) and his pieces have appeared in several industry publications, like the Volta NY 2012 Press Summary.
